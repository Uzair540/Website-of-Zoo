What Items Are Prohibited Or Restricted At The Zoo And Safari Park?

  • Alcohol, marijuana (including marijuana-enriched products), and any illegal substances
  • Any transportation device not otherwise approved as a mobility device (e.g. motorcycles, motorized/non-motorized scooters, trailer-like objects pushed or towed by an ECV)
  • AM/FM radios or any type of music player
  • Attire depicting pictures or words that may be considered offensive or profane to other guests
  • Balloons, balls, Frisbees, jump ropes
  • Any throwing/kicking toy
  • Bug nets

    • How Much Is Parking At The Zoo/Safari Park?

    The Zoo has free parking. The parking lot in front of the Zoo is owned and operated by the City of San Diego and is used by guests, volunteers and employees of Balboa Park.
